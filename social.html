The Journey So Far

After years of avoiding social media and focusing purely on my riding, I finally took the plunge and joined TikTok on 20th November 2025. It was a decision I'd been putting off for a long time‚ÄîI was nervous about putting myself out there and wasn't sure anyone would be interested in following along.

I couldn't have been more wrong. The response from the equestrian community has been absolutely incredible and completely overwhelming. Within just one week, thousands of people have joined me on this journey, and the messages of support and encouragement have been beyond anything I could have imagined.

I'm determined to grow this platform as part of my journey to become a professional show jumper. Every follower, every view, and every bit of support brings me one step closer to that dream. This is just the beginning.

A Note on Kindness

Thank You

The response since joining TikTok has been truly overwhelming. Every comment, every message, every word of encouragement has meant more to me than I can express. This community has welcomed me with open arms, and I'm so grateful for each and every one of you.

Kindness Matters

Social media can be an incredible place for connection, support, and shared passion‚Äîbut only when we choose kindness. I believe we all have a responsibility to make the online space a positive one. Behind every account is a real person with real feelings.

I'm committed to keeping my corner of the internet a welcoming, supportive place. Whether you're here for the horses, the journey, or just to say hello‚Äîthank you for being part of this community and for spreading positivity along the way.
