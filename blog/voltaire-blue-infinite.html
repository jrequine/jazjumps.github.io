Why I Chose the Voltaire Blue Infinite Saddle

One of the most common questions I get asked on TikTok is about my saddle. So I thought it was time to properly introduce you to the Voltaire Blue Infinite‚Äîthe saddle that's changed how I ride and train with DJ.

What is the Blue Infinite?

The Voltaire Blue Infinite isn't just a saddle‚Äîit's a smart saddle. Built on Voltaire Design's reputation for exceptional craftsmanship, the Blue Infinite takes things further by incorporating technology that tracks and analyses your riding in real-time. It's designed specifically for show jumping, and it shows in every aspect of its design.

At its core, it's still a beautifully made French saddle with that classic close-contact feel that jumpers need. But it's what's built into the panels that makes it special.

The Smart Technology

The Blue Infinite features integrated sensors that connect to an app on your phone. It tracks everything from your symmetry in the saddle to your position during jumps. After each ride, you get detailed insights into:

  • Rider symmetry and balance
  • Time in each gait
  • Jump analysis and stride data
  • Training progress over time

For someone like me who's always looking for marginal gains and ways to improve, this data is invaluable. It's like having a coach watching every session, picking up on things you might not notice yourself.

Why It Works for DJ and Me

When I first tried the Blue Infinite, the difference was immediate. The close-contact design puts you right there with the horse‚Äîyou can feel every movement, every adjustment DJ makes. For a horse as responsive as he is, that connection is everything.

The custom fitting process was thorough. Voltaire don't just measure your horse once and hope for the best. They consider how the saddle will sit during movement, during jumps, and how it'll work as your horse's shape changes with fitness and training. For DJ, who's built up significantly since we started working together, that adaptability matters.

The Data-Driven Difference

What I love most is using the app data to inform our training. I can see patterns I'd never have spotted otherwise. For instance, I noticed I was sitting slightly heavier on my left side‚Äîsomething that was affecting DJ's straightness on approaches. Once I could see it in the data, I could work on correcting it.

It's also brilliant for tracking progress. Looking back over months of riding data, I can see our improvements in black and white. That's incredibly motivating, especially during those winter months when progress can feel slow.

Is It Worth the Investment?

Let's be honest‚Äîthe Blue Infinite isn't cheap. It's a premium saddle with premium technology. But for anyone serious about improving their riding and competing at higher levels, I think it's worth every penny.

The combination of exceptional saddle quality and the insights from the technology has genuinely made me a better rider. And ultimately, that makes DJ's job easier too. When I'm balanced and in the right position, he can do his best work.

Final Thoughts

The Voltaire Blue Infinite has become an essential part of our setup. It's not just about having a nice saddle‚Äîit's about having the tools to constantly improve. As we head into the 2026 season with the Sunshine Tour on the horizon, every advantage counts.
