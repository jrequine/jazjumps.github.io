About Jasmine

Growing up in a small village in Suffolk, I developed a passion for horses from a young age. Now a semi-professional show jumper with over 20 years of experience working with horses, every day is still dedicated to training, improving, and chasing that perfect clear round. Family means everything to me, and I'm lucky to have an amazing boyfriend from Florida who supports me every step of the way.
