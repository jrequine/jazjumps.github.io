About Jasmine

Growing up in a small village in Suffolk, I developed a passion for horses from a young age. Now a semi-professional show jumper with over 20 years of experience working with horses, every day is still dedicated to training, improving, and chasing that perfect clear round. In 2026, I'll be joining the Andalucia Sunshine Tour in Spain alongside a full competition schedule across the United Kingdom and events throughout Europe. Read about my 2026 plans.

I also run Jasmine's Equine Transport (JET), a fully insured professional horse transport business.
