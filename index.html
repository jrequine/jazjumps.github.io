About Jasmine

Growing up in a small village in Suffolk, I developed a passion for horses from a young age. Now a semi-professional show jumper with over 20 years of experience working with horses, every day is still dedicated to training, improving, and chasing that perfect clear round. In 2026, I'll be joining the Sunshine Tour in Spain alongside a full competition schedule across the United Kingdom and events throughout Europe. Family means everything to me, and I'm lucky to have an amazing boyfriend from Florida who supports me every step of the way.

I also run Jasmine Equine Transport (JET), a fully insured professional horse transport business. With years of experience working at Newmarket's finest yards and studs, I've driven over 450,000 miles across England and beyond‚Äîincluding European ferry crossings and international air transport. I specialise in transporting mares to be covered along with their foals at foot.
